About Me

Growing up in a bicultural, immigrant household, I was enthralled by the stories shared of my distant relatives in Turkey and my parents’ experiences in the United States. Their anecdotes allowed me to access history I felt deeply connected to, even without experiencing the events firsthand. Each conversation left me wanting more, so I kept questioning, kept being eager for any crumbs of information about my heritage and culture.

My early fascination didn't end with childhood stories and instead evolved into the driving force behind my path as a young journalist today. This curiosity that once compelled me to listen intently at my yearly family gatherings now motivates me to investigate complex issues and perspectives often overlooked, while understanding the humanity behind them. Journalism has become an expression of my desire to question and explore the stories that shape not just me, but the world.
