The Mastery of Michael Phelps



5x Olympic swimmer Michael Phelps is most known for his record of most Olympic gold medals held of any swimmer, where he earned eight gold medals at the 2008 Olympic games in Beijing, China. He has 28 Olympic medals total spanning throughout his 16-year career, with 23 being gold



He began his Olympic career at age 15, at the 2000 Olympic games in Sydney, Australia. He competed in his final games in 2016 in Rio De Janeiro, Brazil. Phelps also created his own organization, called the Michael Phelps Foundation, aiming to provide water safety education and wellness programs to kids.
